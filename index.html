About Us

We are the student-led China Studies Society at King's College London, affiliated with the Lau China Institute.

Whether you specialise in China studies or simply wish to understand its role in our globalised world, our society is for you. We move beyond stereotypes and simplistic narratives to explore China’s history, culture, politics, economy and global influence through genuine student perspectives.

We host accessible, thoughtful events including talks, panel discussions and cultural gatherings. We engage directly with academics, diplomats and industry experts to create informed, respectful dialogue for our members.

Supported by the Lau China Institute, we connect our members with leading voices in China‑related fields while building an interdisciplinary community of globally minded students. We aim to broaden perspectives, challenge simplistic narratives and encourage constructive conversation for anyone seeking a clearer understanding of China in the modern world.
